Project Overview

The "Movie Ticket Booking System" is an advanced web-based platform that enables users to book movie tickets online with ease. It simplifies the ticket reservation process and enhances the movie-watching experience by providing a seamless and user-friendly interface.

1. Purpose and Goal: The primary goal of this project is to provide an efficient online movie ticket booking system where users can browse movies, select showtimes, and book tickets effortlessly. It minimizes manual efforts and enhances convenience.

2. Problem Addressed: Traditional ticket booking involves long queues and manual processes. This system eliminates these challenges by offering an automated online solution that ensures quick and secure bookings.

3. Target Audience: The platform is designed for movie lovers who prefer booking tickets online, cinema operators who need an efficient system to manage bookings, and administrators who can oversee transactions.
